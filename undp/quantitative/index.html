Does Good Governance Affect
Inequality in Sub-Saharan Africa?
This visualization compares the level good governance in Sub-Saharan African countries with their level of inequality and aims to identify correlations between the two, e.g. does better control of corruption affect inequality? It uses indicators from the Worldwide Governance Indicators (WGI) 2015, which measures six dimensions of governance: voice and accountability, politically stability and absence of violence/terrorism, government effectiveness, regulatory quality, rule of law, and control of corruption and compares them with the latest Gini available from that country.
  • WGI 2015: Voice and Accountability
  • WGI 2015: Political Stability
  • WGI 2015: Government Effectiveness
  • WGI 2015: Regualtory Quality
  • WGI 2015: Rule of Law
  • WGI 2015: Control of Corruption
